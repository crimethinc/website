#2: Work and the Anarchist Critique of Capitalism

Summary

Why is this podcast called “the Ex-Worker”? In this episode, hosts Alanis and Clara discuss work and how it works with a friend in a cafe, in the first installment of our series exploring anarchist critiques of capitalism. Episode two also features a special report on Mayday actions around the world, a review of Silvia Federici's “Caliban and the Witch,” an interview and reportback from a Canadian anarcha-feminist conference, and plenty more. {May 19th, 2013}
